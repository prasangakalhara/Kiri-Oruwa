Strategic Vision

To transform Kiri-Oruwa into a premier destination for Slow Tourism, leveraging the authentic rural lifestyle of Kandegethera. The project avoids mass tourism in favor of a "high-value, low-impact" model that integrates European travelers directly into the village lifecycle—paddy harvesting, cinnamon peeling, and traditional cooking.

Unique Value Proposition

  • Authenticity: Not a hotel, but a curated village residence.
  • Sustainability: 100% solar daytime power, zero-plastic zones.
  • Community: 40% of net profits reinvested in local infrastructure.
